Meet the Groomers

Our team of highly trained experts is committed to providing the best grooming experience for you and your pets

Anna, owner & master groomer

Born and raised in the PNW, Anna has 12 years of experience in grooming dogs and other pets. She was educated at Maser's Academy of Fine Grooming in Kenmore, and is NDGAA-certified in both non-sporting and sporting dogs.
